> Durango Colorado has one of the best mountain bike trail systems anywhere! Our intermediate tour will showcase awesome singletrack found at lower elevations than our Colorado Trail Tour, yet we'll still ride some of the best trails this remarkable area offers, including Hermosa Creek Trail, parts of the Colorado Trail and a mostly all day descent to Junction Creek. We return each evening to a comfortable base camp located at Junction Creek.
