Join us for this memorable sunrise tour, complete with a delicious continental-style breakfast, unparalleled scenic vistas and a fun easy route through amazing desert terrain, including some sections of slickrock. This tour is perfect for those without a lot of mountain biking experience. That said, all riders need to be comfortable using hand brakes as well as be fit enough to pedal up some rising terrain. It will include about 2.5 hours of pedaling time. Four person minimum required for this tour. This tour is offered on a custom basis only. Please call or email for availability.
