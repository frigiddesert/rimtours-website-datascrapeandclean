**Long Description:**
> Ride southwestern Utah's newest slickrock and singletrack trails! Our tour explores the fantastic network of trails found on Gooseberry Mesa and beyond. The technical and challenging riding weaves through varied terrain of open slickrock and pinyon/juniper forests. The views are spectacular with the dramatic backdrop of Zion National Park in the distance. The Gooseberry Mesa Tour offers experienced riders a perfect opportunity to test their singletrack skills in the early spring or late fall at a comfortable elevation around 5,000 ft.
