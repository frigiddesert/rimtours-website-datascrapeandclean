## 1. The Shared DNA (Content)
*This description applies to ALL variations of Ebike Intro Tour.*

**Short Description:**
> The aim of this tour is to offer a fun learning experience while riding our full-suspension, pedal assist ebikes. If you are newer to mt biking and want to get a taste of all the fun of a Class 1 ebike, then come join us! 

 <br><br>

We will give you a detailed briefing on riding safety and techniques, as well as some practice in the parking area prior to getting out on the tour’s scenic adventure. Along the way, we will discuss, demonstrate, and assist you with any riding questions or difficulties. You will get to ride on terrain that will mostly undulate on a mix of packed dirt, sandstone and rocky terrain – all the favorite features of Moab-style riding! So come on out and join the FUN!
