Tour Rating: Intermediate- Advanced. Physical demand is intermediate to
strenuous.

Mileage: Approximately 15-25 miles per day


Terrain: Singletrack trails on dirt and rock.


Technical Difficulty: Challenging sections of singletrack including narrow
trails with obstacles, small ledges, short steep grades, tight switchbacks
and several sections of exposed trail.


Altitude: 4,500 to 5,000 ft.

Important: A hydration pack with at least a 100 oz. (3L) bladder and enough
room to carry extra layers, some gear, and food is strongly recommended.
If you prefer a fanny or daypack we recommend you bring a pack with the
ability to carry at least 2 large water bottles, lunch, and extra clothing.
