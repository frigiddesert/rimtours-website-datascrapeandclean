**Long Description:**
> On this new tour we will use e-bikes to explore the best Singletracks along the route of The Kokopelli Trail from Fruita, CO to Moab, UT. Backcountry campsites allow ample time to take in the majestic corridor of the Colorado river at the Utah/CO border.

We'll then make our way towards "Rabbit Valley" and the Western Rim trail, traversing the rock rims above the Colorado River, the sometimes technical and narrow trails climb and descend through high desert terrain with expansive vistas towards Moab.

Today we will pack our camp and head back towards Grand Junction to ride at the Third Flats trails.
