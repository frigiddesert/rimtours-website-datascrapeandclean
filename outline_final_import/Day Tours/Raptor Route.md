**Short Description:**
> Raptor Route - Eagle Eye, Hawks Glide, Falcon Flow and Kestrel run is a 12 mile popular intermediate route located near Moab Utah. Raptor Route trails are near the famous Porcupine rim area, and serve as an easier alternative to enjoy similar terrain. This mountain bike primary route can be used both directions and has a moderate overall physical rating. This route includes 4 trails. This route climbs 497 ft with a max elevation of 7,275 ft then descends -2,661 ft.

**Long Description:**
> This new 12-mile three trail system offers fun, fast, and flowy intermediate singletrack; Eagle Eye, Hawk's Glide, Falcon Flow, and Kestrel run.
