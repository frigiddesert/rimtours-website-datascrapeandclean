**Short Description:**
> This Elementary Ebike tour is geared for riders that want a basic "grasshopper" level. It will be cater to those wanting to get out and enjoy Ebikes in a scenic environment, while giving you a guided stepping stone into the sport.

**Long Description:**
> The focus is to make this a fun and elementary level tour.
We will give you a detailed briefing of riding safety, and techniques, with some practice in the parking area prior to getting out on the tour's scenic adventure.
Along the way, we will discuss, demostrate, and assist you with any riding questions or difficulties.
Our aim is to provide a fun, and learning experience with Ebikes.
