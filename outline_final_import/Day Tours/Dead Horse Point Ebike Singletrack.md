Towering 2,000 feet above the Colorado River, the park provides a breathtaking panorama of the Canyonlands area with sculpted pinnacles and buttes. This super fun intermediate singletrack has rolling terrain, mixed with some short climbs and lots of fun sections to cruise on. Some previous mountain bike experience is highly recommended.
Class 1 Ebikes only at DHP
Brand new Santa Cruz Ebikes will take us on an epic ride, and are included with all tours. The pedal-assist bikes ride just like a regular bike and only power when you pedal.  This natural feeling boost will help you conquer moves like never before and deliver miles of smiles.
Don't let the motor fool you though! All the essential and technical skills of mountain biking still apply, and you will still work up a great sweat.  Deadhorse Point trails are primarily rock at times and require intermediate technical skills and previous off-road mountain bike experience.
