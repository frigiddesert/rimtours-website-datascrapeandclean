**Long Description:**
> This super scenic tour is a great way to experience mountain biking in Moab with vehicle support! There are spectacular views of Canyonlands National Park on the initial descent as we ride a small portion of the famous White Rim Trail. With some challenging climbs, exciting descents and a trailside lunch at Thelma and Louise Point, riders of differing abilities, with good fitness, will enjoy this full day adventure through the red rock country.  Four person minimum required for this tour and is only offered in the Spring and Fall. Please call or email for availability.
