> This ride has it all! Join us for a fast-paced descent on some of the best terrain Moab has to offer. Ledges, drops, and a great section of technical singletrack all combine to make Porcupine Rim an outstanding world-famous ride. While there is a significant overall loss of elevation on this route you will definitely still need to do some pedaling. Don't forget to pause for the remarkable views of Castle Valley, Castleton Tower and the Colorado River along the way! Additional shuttle fees apply.
